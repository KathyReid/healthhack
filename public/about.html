About BRISK

Formed as part of Health Hack Melbourne 2013, BRISK provides a tool for women to explore their risk for breast cancer. Breast cancer risks fall into two distinct categories;

  • Modifiable risks: These risks are able to be moderated through changes to lifestyle and behaviour, such as weight.
  • Non-modifiable risks: These risks are not able to be changed. They are due to genetics and family history.

By better understanding their risk for contracting breast cancer, the tool helps women to make informed health decisions, which may include preventative medication and/or surgery.
